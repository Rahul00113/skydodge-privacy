Privacy Policy for Sky Dodge

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Introduction

Sky Dodge ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile game developed for Android. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information collected during your use of our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game data (scores, progress, settings, coins) is stored locally on your device using an on-device database and is never sent to any external server.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party service:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location data (based on IP)
  • App usage data

For more information on how Google uses data, please review Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses information from apps.

Children's Privacy

Sky Dodge is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The ads displayed may be configured as non-personalized to comply with children's privacy regulations such as COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information.

Data Storage and Retention

All game progress, settings, and coin balances are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to any server.

This data is retained on your device as long as the app is installed. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data. You may also clear the app's data at any time through your device's app settings.

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Internet access: Required solely for loading advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback during gameplay (e.g., collecting coins, collisions). This can be disabled in the app's settings.

Your Rights (GDPR / EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access: Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access.
  • Right to deletion: You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing its data in your device settings.
  • Right to opt out of personalized ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

For any data collected by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy to exercise your rights regarding that data.

Your Rights (CCPA / California Users)

If you are a California resident, under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to know what personal information is collected, request its deletion, and opt out of its sale. Since Sky Dodge does not collect, store, or sell personal information, these rights are satisfied by default. For data handled by Google AdMob, please consult Google's privacy documentation.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
