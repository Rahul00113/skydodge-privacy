Privacy Policy for Sky Dodge

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Introduction

Sky Dodge ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile game developed for Android. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information collected during your use of our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game data (scores, progress, settings, coins) is stored locally on your device using an on-device database and is never sent to any external server.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party service:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location data (based on IP)
  • App usage data

For more information on how Google uses data, please review Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses information from apps.

Children's Privacy

Sky Dodge is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The ads displayed may be configured as non-personalized to comply with children's privacy regulations.

Data Storage

All game progress, settings, and coin balances are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete this data. We have no access to this data.

Permissions

The app requires the following permission:

  • Internet access: Required solely for loading advertisements via Google AdMob.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
